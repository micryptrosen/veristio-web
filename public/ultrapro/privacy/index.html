Privacy Policy — UltraPro

Last Updated: 2025-12-26

UltraPro (“UltraPro,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is designed as an evidence-first network change verification platform. We are committed to respecting user privacy and minimizing data collection.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

UltraPro collects limited, purpose-specific information only to support product functionality, evaluation, and improvement.

During beta use or licensed operation, UltraPro may collect:

  • Execution metadata (command invocation, timestamps, module usage)
  • High-level environment descriptors (e.g., selected vendor ecosystem)
  • User-provided inputs explicitly entered into the application
  • Optional feedback submitted by users

UltraPro does not collect:

  • Network payload data
  • Authentication credentials
  • Device configuration files beyond user-supplied artifacts
  • Packet captures
  • Customer data transiting monitored networks
  • Continuous monitoring telemetry

How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Verify software functionality
  • Improve accuracy and reliability
  • Diagnose errors and defects
  • Evaluate feature usage during beta testing

Information is not used for advertising, profiling, or resale.

Data Retention

Execution metadata is retained only for the duration of the beta program and internal evaluation unless otherwise required by law.

Retention policies for licensed releases may evolve and will be disclosed prior to any change.

Data Sharing

UltraPro does not sell or share collected information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To trusted service providers operating under confidentiality obligations

Security

UltraPro employs reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect collected information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

Users may:

  • Decline participation in beta programs
  • Request deletion of beta feedback submissions
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
